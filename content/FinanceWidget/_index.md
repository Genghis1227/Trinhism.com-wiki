# FinanceWidget Documentation Hub

Welcome to the **FinanceWidget** documentation space. FinanceWidget is a lightweight desktop utility designed for traders and long-term investors who want clear, continuous visibility of market charts and ticker data directly on their Windows workspaces.

---

## 💡 What is FinanceWidget?

FinanceWidget embeds Microsoft Edge WebView2 containers to render Google Finance stock and ETF quote pages. By automatically injecting optimized clean-up stylesheets and scripts, the app strips away headers, search input fields, footers, and distracting sidebars — leaving only the raw, interactive charts and valuation metrics floating borderless on your screen.

---

## 🌟 Why Use FinanceWidget? (Value Proposition)

Keeping multiple browser tabs open just to monitor stock values is resource-heavy and visually cluttered. FinanceWidget provides a clean, focused, HUD-like desktop layout.

*   🖥️ **Distraction-Free Visuals**: Strips Google Finance down to the bare essentials (price, percentage change, timeframe controls, and chart canvas). No search bars, news feeds, or AI panels.
*   📈 **Continuous Market Monitoring**: Create dedicated widgets for your favorite stocks, indices, ETFs, or cryptocurrencies to track market movements at a glance.
*   🔑 **Google Account Integration**: Right-click any widget to sign in to your Google account inside the secure WebView2 container, enabling a much better and more stable layout rendering when using the Google Finance Beta Site.
*   💨 **Low Resource Footprint**: Engineered in native WPF and WebView2, the application consumes minimal CPU and memory compared to standard full-featured web browsers.
*   🎨 **Tailored Customization**: Freely drag, resize, pin to front ("Keep on Top"), or hide window decorations globally to blend the widgets seamlessly with your desktop wallpaper.
